# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Arabic-speaking Alraedah Finance customers reading transactional and awareness emails across mobile and desktop email clients. They need to identify official communication quickly and act without ambiguity.

## Product Purpose

Deliver clear, trustworthy customer communication that protects customers, reinforces official channels, and remains recognizably Alraedah across campaigns.

## Brand Personality

Protective, credible, direct. The experience should feel calm and authoritative without becoming institutional or visually generic.

## Anti-references

Generic fintech layouts, invented brand colors, excessive card stacks, rasterized message copy, decorative effects without a communication purpose, and dark-mode treatments that recolor or corrupt the brand artwork.

## Design Principles

- Preserve the approved Alraedah visual system before introducing new treatments.
- Make the safety message scannable through clear hierarchy and semantic color.
- Keep campaign copy as accessible, selectable HTML text.
- Use imagery only for genuine brand and illustrative assets.
- Treat light and dark mode as one resilient email implementation.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Maintain RTL reading order, selectable text, descriptive links, sufficient contrast, responsive type, and stable rendering in light and dark modes. Avoid relying on color alone for meaning.
